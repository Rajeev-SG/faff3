# Deliverables

These are the main outputs for the faff3 team.

## Files

- `partner-service-catalogue.md`
  The most shareable business document. Start here if the goal is to discuss packages, pricing, and how the proposition sounds to a business owner.
- `mckinsey-style-memo.md`
  The strategic rationale behind the offer, including ICP, positioning, and go-to-market recommendations.

## Suggested use

- Use the service catalogue in partner conversations and early commercial discussions.
- Use the memo when deciding how faff3 should position itself and what to prioritise over the next 90 days.
